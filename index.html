GreenWave Online Store

Online Dropshipping Business

Mission: To provide eco-friendly products that help people live sustainably.

Vision: To become a leading online retailer of eco-conscious products worldwide.

Business Idea

  • Eco-friendly products like reusable bottles, bamboo utensils, biodegradable bags.
  • Unique selling point: Affordable, sustainable products delivered to your door.
  • Target audience: Environment-conscious people, 18–40 years old, online shoppers.

Market Analysis

  • Trend: Eco-friendly products growing globally.
  • Competitors: EcoRoots, Package Free Shop, EarthHero
  • Opportunity: Affordable sustainable products for everyday use
  • Challenge: Competing with established brands

Marketing Strategy

  • Social media campaigns (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook)
  • Influencer partnerships & eco bloggers
  • Email newsletters & promotions
  • SEO optimized website

Operations Plan

  • Products sourced via dropshipping
  • Orders fulfilled directly by suppliers
  • Tools: Shopify, Canva, Mailchimp

Financial Plan

  • Startup Costs: Website $150, Marketing $200, Branding $50
  • Revenue Model: $5–$15 profit per product
  • Expenses: Marketing $100/month, Website $29/month

Goals & Milestones

  • Short-term (1–3 months): Launch website, 50 sales
  • Medium-term (6–12 months): Build social media, 500+ sales
  • Long-term (1–3 years): Expand products, 5000+ annual sales

Conclusion

  • Combines sustainability with convenience
  • Strong market potential with eco-conscious consumers
  • Next Steps: Launch website, start marketing, track performance
